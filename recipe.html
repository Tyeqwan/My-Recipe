How To Make Snickerdoodles

Recipe By Taste of Home Test Kitchen

  • 1/2 cup butter,soften
  • 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons sugar,divided
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • teaspoon ground cinnamon
  1. 1. Preheat oven to 375Â°. Cream butter and 1 cup sugar until light and fluffy; beat in egg and vanilla. In another bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda and cream of tartar; gradually beat into creamed mixture.
  2. 2. In a small bowl, mix cinnamon and remaining 2 Tbsp. sugar. Shape dough into 1-in. balls; roll in cinnamon sugar. Place 2 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  3. 3. Bake until light brown, 10-12 minutes. Remove from pans to wire racks to cool.
